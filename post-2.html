Spam Musubi: The Utlmate Snack

Jared Garalde on

April 26, 2022

Spam Musubis is one of my favorite foods. For anyone who hasn’t heard of it, it’s a rectangle patty of rice, layered with spam, and wrapped in seaweed. It’s especially popular in Hawaii, where I would vacation every year with my family. They’re sold pretty much everywhere on the islands but everyone makes it differently. However, the best ones are the ones that are made at home and you can make almost 20 on just a few dollars. Here’s a little recipe on how to make them! (The Spam might be intimidating but don’t knock it til you try it)!

For supplies, you can use a musubi mold or do it by hand, if you dare!

Ingredients (Serves 10)

  • 1 Can of SPAM
  • 2 Cups of Rice
  • 1 Pack of Seaweed

Ingredients for Marinade(Serves 10)

  • 1 cup of Soy Sauce
  • 1 cuo of Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Cook the Rice
  2. Slice the spam horizontally do desired amount
  3. Cut the seaweed sheetsin half
  4. Mix the brown sugar and soy sauce together until the brown sugar dissolves
  5. Marinate the spam in the mixture for a few minutes
  6. Heat up the pan on medium
  7. Once marinated, place the spam onto the heated pan and fry until fully cooked
