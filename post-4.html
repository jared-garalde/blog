Burning Out: When to Take Breaks

Jared Garalde on

April 24, 2022

One of the things I struggle most with is knowing when to stop. I mean when there's so much to be done, how can you take the time to just chill?

But then I realized, this is a contradiction.

I started to realize that when I push myself too far, I tend to be les proud of my work. I do way less in more time. Go figure.

I started to pick up meditation, and boy, did that help me out. I think slowing down helps me to focus, and it has brought me peace.
